About us                    

We specialize in creative and cost effective geotechnical solutions.
Our engineering services include geotechnical site investigations, seismic site classification, excavation and shoring design, foundations, hydrogeology, earth structures, hazard assessments, and construction monitoring.
We provide technical services including: Materials testing of soils, aggregate, and asphalt, geophysical surveys and utility location, and Erosion and Sediment Control (ESC) design and monitoring.

Our history

Geopacific Consulting has started as a small company in 1985. We have experienced rapid expansion ever since. Currently, we operate in Vancouver, Squamish, Kamploops and Calgary. Our growth was fueled by creative and effective geotechnical services we provide and rests on the reputation we have built.

Our team

GeoPacific employs a staff of approximately 70 people across our offices, including professional engineers and geoscientists, EITs, GITs, technologists, technicians, drafters, and administrative staff. Our team is a diverse group, with varying levels of experience from Canada and abroad. We strive to build a team and work environment that promotes collaboration, innovation, and trust.
